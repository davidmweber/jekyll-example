Agile Coach/ScrumMaster

Not just any ScrumMaster. We are looking for the program’s heart beat and orchestrator-in-chief. Passionate about growing new developers in Africa, experienced agile coach, great if you can code a thing or two. Networker, people person, committed to developing Jo’burg's tech ecosystem. Manage--and inspire--teams of new developers to think on their feet and develop their own voices and brains. Sensitive to their progress as coders and as thinkers. Able to handle the many non-tech related issues that come up. Lead standups and retrospectives, and keep the agile rhythm. Interface with industry sponsors on their projects, product owners, coordinate mentor talks. Take active role in matching new projects to skills learned, encourage and evaluate students on an ongoing basis. Make sure everything runs--including the office itself--coders are happy and challenged, new projects come in the door and top quality solutions get delivered.

Applicants need to have at least 5 years of relevant industrial experience including at least 2 years of scrum experience, group facilitation and management responsibilities. Competitive salary.

Code Mentor

Experienced multifaceted agile developer with a passion for teaching. You are on hand to help students learn how to learn to be awesome developers. You can break complex concepts down into digestible chunks. As they develop, you guide them towards discovering their own solutions. Patience, kindness, sense of humor are musts. Versatility and creativity in thinking about shaping projects that meet client needs and attain critical industry skills, deep knowledge of open source stacks, software and hardware.

Applicants need 5 years of solid experience in the software industry as a developer/software engineer. They should have worked in a broad range of technologies, managed medium sized team, comfortable with multiple languages and operating systems, and must have a track record of mentoring junior staff. Competitive salary.
