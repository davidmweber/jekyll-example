Want a job in tech but don't have training?
Programmers are in high demand and critically short supply. codeX is a brand new program training talented young brains to get these high paying jobs, and build Africa's digital future.

Technical skills can be learned, you just need to be a self-motivated thinker. If you believe you’ll always be learning and will stop at nothing to solve the problem at hand, we want you. 

How does it work?
codeX is a full-time apprenticeship 9 to 5, every weekday. Very quickly, you'll build real projects for real companies. codeX guides you and gives you a safe space to experiment and grow. The first term runs full time from 15 September to 12 December 2014.

Sponsorships
The cost is R25 000 per three month term. We also have sponsorships available for deserving applicants (so show us what you can do)!

How do you apply?
Complete our online form with your details, something you’ve made (anything - it doesn’t need to be code!) and some interesting puzzles. 

Then join codeX at TEDx
We want to get to know you and see what you can do in person. 
Top applicants will be invited to our "hackathon" at Cape Town's TEDx conference on Saturday 16 August, from 11:00 am - 3:00pm. 
You'll work on teams to build projects (no tech skills needed!), just like you'll be doing every day if you get into codeX.

All qualifying applicants will also be able to attend TEDx talks in the opening and closing session free of charge!
